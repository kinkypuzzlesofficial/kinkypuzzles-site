Frequently Asked Questions

Is packaging discreet?

Yes, orders ship in plain, unmarked packaging so your purchase remains private.

What are your shipping rates?

Standard shipping within the United States starts at $9.95 for the first item and $1.50 for each additional item. Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping. Handling fees are included in these rates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards and PayPal for fast and secure checkout.

Can I return a used toy?

For hygiene reasons, used toys cannot be returned. Unopened products may be returned within 30 days of delivery subject to our returns policy.
